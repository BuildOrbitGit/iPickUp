Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 27, 2026  ·  iPickUp · Kareem Khan, Greater Vancouver & Fraser Valley, B.C.

Short version: When you request a quote, your name and phone number are sent directly to Kareem. He calls or texts you back. Your info is not stored on any web server. It is not sold or shared with anyone. The only third party involved is FormSubmit.co — a relay service that delivers your form to Kareem's email. They do not store your data.

1. Who We Are & Who Is Responsible

iPickUp is a truck-for-hire service based in Greater Vancouver and the Fraser Valley, British Columbia, Canada, operated by Kareem Khan. Kareem Khan is the person responsible for privacy at iPickUp.

This policy is governed by British Columbia's Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA, SBC 2003) and, where applicable, the federal Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA).

2. What Information We Collect

When you submit the quote request form on our website, you provide your name, phone number, the service you need (e.g., delivery, towing, junk removal), and optional job details. This is the only personal information we collect through this website.

3. Purpose of Collection

We collect your name and phone number for one reason: so Kareem can call or text you back to discuss your job and provide a quote. Your information is not used for any other purpose.

We do not send marketing texts, emails, or promotional messages. If we ever wish to contact you for promotional purposes, we will obtain your separate, express consent as required by Canada's Anti-Spam Legislation (CASL).

4. Consent

By submitting the quote request form, you consent to us collecting and using your contact information to respond to your request. You may withdraw your consent at any time by contacting Kareem directly.

5. How Your Data Is Transmitted — FormSubmit.co

This website uses FormSubmit.co, a third-party form delivery service, to transmit your quote request to Kareem's email inbox. When you submit the form:

  • Your name, phone number, service type, and any message are sent to FormSubmit.co's servers.
  • FormSubmit.co relays this information to Kareem's email address.
  • FormSubmit.co does not permanently store your personal information — it acts as a relay, not a database.
  • FormSubmit.co's own privacy policy can be found at formsubmit.co.

Aside from FormSubmit.co's relay function, we do not operate any server or database that stores your personal information.

Google Fonts: This website loads fonts from Google's servers (fonts.googleapis.com). When your browser requests these fonts, Google may log your IP address as part of standard web font delivery. We do not control or access this data. See Google's Privacy Policy at google.com/policies/privacy.

6. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, share, or disclose your personal information to any third parties, advertisers, or data brokers. FormSubmit.co is a technical relay service only — they are not a recipient of your information for any other purpose.

7. Retention

Once Kareem receives your quote request by email and the job inquiry is resolved (quote given, job completed, or inquiry closed), your contact details are retained only as long as needed for follow-up — typically no more than 90 days. After that, they are deleted.

8. Security Safeguards

We use security measures appropriate to the information we hold:

  • Form submissions are transmitted over HTTPS (encrypted in transit) through FormSubmit.co.
  • Kareem's email account is password-protected and accessible only to him.
  • We do not store your information on shared or unprotected devices.

No internet transmission is 100% secure. We take reasonable precautions but cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Cookies & Tracking

This website does not use tracking cookies, analytics software, or third-party monitoring tools beyond the Google Fonts service described in Section 5. We do not track how you browse this site.

10. Your Rights Under PIPA BC

Under British Columbia's Personal Information Protection Act, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we hold about you.
  • Request access to your personal information.
  • Request corrections if information is inaccurate.
  • Withdraw your consent.
  • Make a complaint about how we handle your information.

Contact Kareem using the details below. We will respond within 30 days.
